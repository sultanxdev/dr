FAQs Around Best Dermatologist in Indore

Do I need an appointment to visit Skintimacy or can I walk in?

You’re welcome to walk in at Skintimacy®, but we recommend booking an appointment in advance for a smoother, more personalized experience with minimal wait time. Appointments can be easily booked through our website (www.drdishabaxi.com), by phone (+91-93295 19520), or via email (contact@skintimacy.in). We’re here to make your visit comfortable and convenient.

What are the consultation and follow-up charges at Skintimacy?

At Skintimacy®, the initial consultation with Dr. Disha Baxi is ₹500, and it’s valid for 7 days to allow time for observation or any early adjustments. If you need to return after 7 days, the follow-up consultation charge is ₹350. We aim to offer continued, personalized care in a way that’s accessible and supportive throughout your treatment journey.

Is Skintimacy a unisex skin clinic in Indore?

Yes, Skintimacy® is a fully unisex skin clinic in Indore, welcoming clients of all genders with equal warmth and expertise. What payment options are available at Skintimacy?

At Skintimacy®, we accept a wide range of payment methods for your convenience. You can pay using credit cards, debit cards, UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, etc.), and cash. Our goal is to make your visit seamless — right from consultation to checkout.

Are treatments at Skintimacy covered under insurance?

Currently, dermatological and aesthetic treatments are not covered under health insurance in India, so procedures at Skintimacy® are typically self-paid. We ensure transparent pricing and personalized treatment plans to help you make informed decisions about your skin and hair care. However, we do provide detailed tax invoices in case you wish to show the expense for reimbursement or personal record-keeping.
